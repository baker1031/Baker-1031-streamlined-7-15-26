The tax on a Denver sale

Denver FAQ
What's the typical home value in Denver? The typical Denver home value is about $559,000 (-3.6% year-over-year, per Zillow's home value index, 2026). Owners of appreciated Denver rental or investment property often use a 1031 exchange into a DST to defer tax and move to passive ownership. What tax would I owe selling investment property in Denver? Denver sits in Colorado, which taxes gains as ordinary income up to 4.4%; with the federal 20% rate and 3.8% NIIT, the combined bill can reach ~28.2%. A 1031 exchange into a DST defers it.
