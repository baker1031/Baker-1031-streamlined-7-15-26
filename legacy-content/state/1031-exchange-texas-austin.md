The tax on a Austin sale
Texas has no state income tax, so a Austin sale is taxed only federally — 20% long-term capital gains plus the 3.8% net investment income tax, about 23.8% of the gain. A 1031 exchange into a DST defers all of it.

Austin FAQ
What's the typical home value in Austin? The typical Austin home value is about $513,000 (-6.8% year-over-year, per Zillow's home value index, 2026). Owners of appreciated Austin rental or investment property often use a 1031 exchange into a DST to defer tax and move to passive ownership. Do Austin sellers owe state tax on a gain? No. Texas has no state income tax, so a Austin property sale is taxed only federally — up to 23.8% on the gain — which a 1031 exchange can defer. Does a DST have to be located in Austin? No. A DST doesn't have to hold Austin (or even Texas) property — you can exchange your Austin sale proceeds into vetted DST offerings anywhere in the country.
