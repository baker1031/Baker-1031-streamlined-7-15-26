The tax on a Houston sale
Texas has no state income tax, so a Houston sale is taxed only federally — 20% long-term capital gains plus the 3.8% net investment income tax, about 23.8% of the gain. A 1031 exchange into a DST defers all of it.

Houston FAQ
What's the typical home value in Houston? The typical Houston home value is about $265,000 (-0.4% year-over-year, per Zillow's home value index, 2026). Owners of appreciated Houston rental or investment property often use a 1031 exchange into a DST to defer tax and move to passive ownership. Do Houston sellers owe state tax on a gain? No. Texas has no state income tax, so a Houston property sale is taxed only federally — up to 23.8% on the gain — which a 1031 exchange can defer. Does a DST have to be located in Houston? No. A DST doesn't have to hold Houston (or even Texas) property — you can exchange your Houston sale proceeds into vetted DST offerings anywhere in the country.
