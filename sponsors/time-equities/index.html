Overview

Time Equities is a New York diversified firm, founded in 1966 by Francis Greenburger, that pioneered cooperative and condominium conversions—more than 10,000 units over its history—and now develops large mixed-use towers such as 50 West Street and Chicago's 1000M, alongside a portfolio exceeding $5.4 billion and 36 million square feet. Diversified across 33 states and international markets and holding more than 5,000 multifamily units, the firm pairs a long-hold value orientation with genuine development capability. Its breadth and longevity make it a seasoned, diversified operator rather than a single-sector DST house.

Key strategies & advantages

  • Pioneered cooperative and condominium conversions (10,000+ units historically).
  • Develops large mixed-use towers such as 50 West Street and Chicago's 1000M.
  • Diversified across 33 states and international markets.
  • Holds 5,000+ multifamily units within a $5.4B+, 36M-sq-ft portfolio.
  • Pursues a patient long-hold value orientation under founder Francis Greenburger.
These describe the sponsor’s approach and potential advantages only, and should be weighed against the risks common to DST investments — illiquidity, loss of principal, and no guaranteed distributions. Full risk factors are in each offering’s Private Placement Memorandum.
