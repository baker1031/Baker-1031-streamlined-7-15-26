Overview

Syndicated Equities is a Chicago private real estate firm with a 40-year history, distinguished by strength in government/GSA-leased and credit-tenant net-lease assets and by its global 'Access Funds' fund-of-funds vehicles. Citing roughly $2 billion in asset value, $600 million in equity and more than $4 billion acquired over its lifetime, the firm has returned $750 million-plus to investors and built multi-decade repeat relationships. The government-credit net-lease specialization is among the most defensive available, and the fund-of-funds capability adds an institutional dimension uncommon at its size.

Key strategies & advantages

  • Strong in government/GSA-leased and credit-tenant net lease, among the most defensive profiles.
  • Brings a 40-year operating history, deep seasoning for a private firm.
  • Operates global 'Access Funds' fund-of-funds vehicles, an institutional capability uncommon at its size.
  • Has returned $750M+ to investors over its history.
  • Maintains multi-decade repeat relationships; manages ~$2 billion in asset value.
These describe the sponsor’s approach and potential advantages only, and should be weighed against the risks common to DST investments — illiquidity, loss of principal, and no guaranteed distributions. Full risk factors are in each offering’s Private Placement Memorandum.
