Overview

HPA Exchange is the DST platform affiliated with Housing Partnership Acquisitions, specializing in single-family rental (SFR) portfolios—an institutionalizing asset class that brings scaled residential operations to exchange investors. Its differentiation is the SFR focus and the operating platform behind it; current AUM is not publicly disclosed, and the firm's limited public footprint warrants direct diligence.

Key strategies & advantages

  • Specializes in single-family rental (SFR) portfolios, an institutionalizing residential asset class.
  • Operates a scaled SFR platform, differentiated from apartment or net-lease DSTs.
  • Affiliated with Housing Partnership Acquisitions for operating infrastructure.
  • Offers exposure to dispersed single-family assets rather than a single building.
  • Current AUM is not publicly disclosed.
These describe the sponsor’s approach and potential advantages only, and should be weighed against the risks common to DST investments — illiquidity, loss of principal, and no guaranteed distributions. Full risk factors are in each offering’s Private Placement Memorandum.
