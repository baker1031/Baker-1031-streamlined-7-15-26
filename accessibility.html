Accessibility Statement

Baker 1031 Investments is committed to making this website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability. Last updated: June 2026.

Our commitment Our commitment Baker 1031 Investments is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities and to continually improving the experience for everyone, applying relevant accessibility standards as the site evolves.

Standard Conformance standard We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, which explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities. We consider the site to be partially conformant: some parts may not yet fully meet the standard, and we are actively working to improve them.

Measures Measures we take Semantic HTML structure and descriptive headings. Meaningful alternative text for images. Keyboard navigability with visible focus. Sufficient color contrast and responsive, resizable text. Ongoing review as content and technology change, with accessibility considered in design and procurement.

Limitations Known limitations Some third-party or downloadable content — such as sponsor PDFs and offering documents — may not be fully accessible because it is produced by others. If you need any such information in an accessible format, contact us and we will provide an accessible alternative or assistance.

Feedback Feedback & how to reach us If you encounter any barrier to accessing content on this site, or need information in an alternative format, contact us at invest@baker1031.com or +1 415 579 1660 . We welcome your feedback and aim to respond within a few business days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may ask that your concern be escalated for further review.
